Your Journey to eJPT v2 Certification Starts Here

Welcome to your personalized, interactive guide for conquering the eJPTv2 (eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester) certification. This roadmap is designed for beginners, providing a clear, step-by-step plan to build your skills from the ground up. The eJPT is a 100% practical certification that proves you have the skills to be a penetration tester, covering assessment methodologies and the full pentesting workflow. This guide breaks down the complex syllabus into a manageable 4-week schedule. Use the navigation to explore prerequisites, the daily study plan, budget estimates, and helpful resources.

The 4-Week Study Roadmap

This is the core of your preparation. The roadmap is structured into four weeks, each focusing on a specific set of skills from the eJPT syllabus. Each day has a clear checklist of topics to cover and practical tasks to perform. Click on a week to view your daily plan. Consistency is key, so try to stick to the schedule as much as possible.

Week 1: Foundations & Network Auditing

Focus: Building a strong foundation in networking, assessment methodologies, and mastering network scanning with tools like Nmap.

Days 1-2: Assessment Methodologies

Days 3-4: Host & Network Auditing

Days 5-7: Mastering Nmap

Week 2: Enumeration & Web Application Auditing

Focus: Learning to enumerate various services and diving into web application security with Burp Suite.

Days 8-10: Service Enumeration

Days 11-12: Intro to Web Apps & Burp Suite

Days 13-14: Web Attack Vectors

Week 3: Exploitation & Pivoting

Focus: Gaining access to systems using Metasploit, performing password attacks, and learning how to move through a network.

Days 20-21: Pivoting & Data Exfiltration

Week 4: Review, Practice & Final Prep

Focus: Consolidating all your knowledge, practicing in exam-like environments, and preparing your mindset for the exam.

Days 27-28: Final Prep & Rest

Budget Estimation

Here’s a breakdown of the potential costs associated with the eJPTv2 certification. The primary expenses are the official training materials from INE and the exam voucher itself. While there are many free resources, the INE course is specifically tailored to the exam and is highly recommended. The chart below visualizes the cost distribution for the most common path.

Cost Breakdown

  • INE Premium Subscription: ~$399/year (often on sale). This gives you access to the eJPT course materials and all other INE content.
  • Exam Voucher: $249. This is a one-time purchase for an exam attempt.
  • Total Estimated Cost: ~$648 USD

Note: Prices are approximate and can change. Look out for INE sales and bundles which might offer a better deal.

Essential Resources & Tools

Beyond the official INE course, leveraging other platforms and tools will significantly enhance your learning. This section provides a curated list of valuable resources. Familiarize yourself with the core tools, as you will be using them extensively during your practice and in the exam.

Practice Platforms

  • TryHackMe: Excellent for guided, hands-on learning paths.
  • Hack The Box: Great for challenge-based learning on live machines.
  • INE (Official): The primary source for course material and labs.

Core Tools

  • Nmap: Network discovery and security auditing.
  • Metasploit: Exploitation framework.
  • Burp Suite: Web application analysis proxy.
  • Wireshark: Network protocol analyzer.
  • Hydra: Brute-force password cracker.
  • John the Ripper: Offline password cracker.

Communities

  • INE Discord Channel
  • eJPT Subreddit
